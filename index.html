About Me

My name is Ian Rene, and I am 24 years old. I am currently studying at the University of Mindanao. I am currently enrolled in a course in information technology (IT), which focused on the study and application of computers.

My skills in web development are still being developed, and I am still learning. It would be a waste of all the effort our professor has put in if his students did not learn anything. There is always room to improve skills; I just need to make an effort.

My experience in web development has been amazing. Web development is not easy. Why? From my own experience, I have spent a lot of sleepless nights studying web development. There is a lot to familiarize yourself with and memorize about the functionality needed to create a website, such as the basics, the element tags, and much more.

I had a great experience learning web development. There were times when I wanted to give up because of how hard it was, but I did not—kidding aside, "my parents will kill me". Thanks to our wonderful professor, Sir Chan Peñalosa, who taught us the fundamentals and made our journey fun despite the pressure, I enjoyed all of it.
